BRANDS

Brands are to be worn loud and proud! People should be excited to buy from a local brand as they are from the luxury brands of the world! This excitement is created by exposure which local brands tend to not get enough exposure. Allow us to tell your brand’s story and bridge the gap from the consumer to your brand. Your brand is unique and deserve the attention that the luxury brands are getting. I meet with brand owners to learn not only about their brands but also about them as a person. This allows for each brand to be captured in its own uniqueness and essence.

MUSIC

We have to go for the medal with this one! This is where we can allow the true creativity comes through! It is something of beauty when two artists from two different art forms to create together and with music videos we can do just that! With proper planning, we can make your song into a movie, capturing the essence of every word and the mood at every beat hit!

WEDDINGS

At some point or another a woman has dreamed about being a princess throughout their life. In my eyes, a wedding is the closest thing to that. The epitome of a fairytale moment. That being said, it is an absolute necessity that it is captured in all its magic and beauty. With weddings I always meet with the bride and groom beforehand and ask questions not only about the wedding date but about them. How did they meet? What was the moment you couldn’t keep your eyes off one another? When did you know that the other was the one? This allows me to shape each wedding video uniquely and tell the story of each couple.
